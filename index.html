Beauty of Ukraine Through My Eyes

Hello everyone. My name is Milana and I am Ukrainian, who is currently studying and living in Lithuania. My big passion is travelling, especially around my home country - Ukraine. Ukraine is a land of breathtaking landscapes, from the picturesque Carpathian Mountains to the golden fields of wheat stretching across the countryside. Its rich cultural heritage, embodied in vibrant traditions, historic architecture, and warm hospitality, adds to the undeniable beauty that defines Ukraine. I want to bring you with me to the journey, where we will explore the pearls of Ukraine and see it through my eyes, as I will tell and show you everything about my expeience.
